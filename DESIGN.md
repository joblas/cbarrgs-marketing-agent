# Cbarrgs Vibe Design System

A cinematic, artist-focused design system for the Cbarrgs ecosystem. Inspired by Spotify's immersive darkness and Linear's technical precision.

## Visual Principles

1. **Cinematic Immersion**: Deep black backgrounds (#000000) with high-contrast elements.
2. **Neon Accents**: Electric green (#1DB954) and mysterious purple (#BB86FC) accents to guide the eye.
3. **Bold Typography**: Large, impactful sans-serif headings (Inter/Roboto) for artist presence.
4. **Editorial Layout**: Spacious margins and clear hierarchies, like a high-end music magazine.

## Core Tokens

- **Surface**: #000000 (Black)
- **Primary**: #1DB954 (Spotify Green)
- **Secondary**: #BB86FC (Vibe Purple)
- **Text**: #FFFFFF (High Emphasis), #B3B3B3 (Medium Emphasis)
- **Border**: #282828 (Subtle distinction)

## Components

- **Buttons**: Pill-shaped, electric green, with hover scaling effects.
- **Cards**: Glassmorphic effects (backdrop-filter: blur) for document and image previews.
- **Inputs**: Minimal, dark borders, focused with electric green glows.
