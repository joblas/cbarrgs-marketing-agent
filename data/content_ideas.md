# Content Ideas

Living document for TikTok and Instagram Reels ideas.

## "Pieces For You" EP Promo (April)

1.  **Idea:** DAW Breakdown
    - **Format:** Screen recording of Ableton/Logic showing the complex layers of the lead single, transitioning to a high-quality snippet of the final mix.
    - **Caption:** "There are 50 layers of synths in this one section..."
    - **Audio:** Original sound (the track).

2.  **Idea:** Ambient Visuals
    - **Format:** Moody, dark, minimal aesthetic video (driving at night, rain on a window, slow-motion footage of the city) with the new track playing in the background.
    - **Caption:** "POV: You're driving at 2 AM and trying to find meaning."
    - **Audio:** "Pieces For You" audio snippet.

3.  **Idea:** Live Performance Snippet
    - **Format:** High-quality footage from the April 11th Distinction Gallery show.
    - **Caption:** "The energy at the Escondido show was unreal. New EP out April 25th."
    - **Audio:** Live audio.

4.  **Idea:** Merch Tease
    - **Format:** Unboxing the new physical merch that just arrived. Close-ups of the textures and details.
    - **Caption:** "New pieces arrived today. Hitting the shop soon."
    - **Audio:** Trending atmospheric audio or own music.
